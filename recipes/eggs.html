Beet Pickled Eggs

These beet pickled eggs make for a great snack or side for just about any meal. An exceptional combination of flavors to take boiled eggs up a notch!

Ingredients

  • 1 15 ounce jar of beets
  • 12 whole hard boiled eggs
  • 1.5 cups apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan. Cover with water and bring to a boil for 10-12 minutes
  2. Remove from water and run under cold water
  3. Peel the eggs
  4. Dump pickled beets, vinegar, and sugar into a bowl and mix
  5. Add eggs and mix into a jar, stir and store for 24 hours before eating
