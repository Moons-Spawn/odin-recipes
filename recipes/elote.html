Elote

Grilled corn with a coat of butter isn't anything unusual. Add some mayo, cheese, and chili powder on top of the corn, and you'll be amazed with just how good corn can taste. This elote recipe will be a great addition for any summer grillin'.

Ingedients

  • 4 ears of corn, shucked
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup grated cotija cheese
  • 4 wedges of lime (optional)
  • Chili powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for medium-high heat
  2. Grill corn until hot and lightly charred all over, 7-10 minutes
  3. Roll ears in melted butter, then spread evenly with mayonnaise
  4. Sprinkle with cotija cheese and chili powder, serve with a lime wedge
