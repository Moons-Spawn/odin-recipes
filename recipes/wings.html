Sweet Chili Chicken Wings

Chicken wings in general are great, and everyone has their own favorite flavor. These sweet chili and marmalade chicken wings are the perfect if you're looking for something sweet with a nice zing to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wing pieces, drumettes and flats
  • 1/2 cup orange marmalade
  • 1/4 cup sweet chili sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F or 200 C.
  2. Set a rack inside a large foil-lined rimmed baking sheet; arrange chicken wings on top.
  3. Bake until wings are no longer pink at the bone and the skin is crispy (45-50 minutes).
  4. Mix orange marmalade and sweet chili sauce in a large bowl until thoroughly combined. Add cooked wings to marmalade-chili sauce and toss until well coated. Plate wings and pour extra sauce on top.
